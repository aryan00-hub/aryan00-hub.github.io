Regular latte

Best choice if you like coffee flavor.

Vanilla / Chai / Matcha

Vanilla latte: espresso + milk + vanilla syrup.

Chai latte: spiced tea + milk (usually sweeter).

Matcha latte: green tea matcha + milk + honey if sweeter (earthy taste).
